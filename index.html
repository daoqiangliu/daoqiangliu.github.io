About Me

I am currently a third-year Ph.D. student in the School of Mathematical Sciences at Capital Normal University supervised by Prof. Zhenlei Zhang (CNU) and Prof. Bo Liu (co-adviser, ECNU ).

Research Interest

My research interest lies in differential geometry and geometric analysis. In particular, I am interested in studying index theory, minimal surfaces, harmonic functions, and their applications to mathematical general relativity and geometry and topology of positive scalar curvature.
