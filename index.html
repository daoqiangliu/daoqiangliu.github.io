I'm a Postdoc in Chern Institute of Mathematics at Nankai University, hosted by Prof. Weiping Zhang.
My research interests include differential geometry and geometric analysis. My recent work is focused on index theory and its applications to mathematical general relativity and the geometry and topology of positive scalar curvature.
Here is my CV (updated 9/8/24).

I received my PhD at Capital Normal University in 2024, supervised by Prof. Zhenlei Zhang (CNU) and Prof. Bo Liu (ECNU).
I received my undergraduate degree and master degree at the Capital Normal University, where I was also mentored by Prof. Zhenlei Zhang.
