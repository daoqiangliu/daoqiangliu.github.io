About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the School of Mathematical Sciences at Capital Normal University since the fall of 2021, supervised by Prof. Zhenlei Zhang (CNU) and Prof. Bo Liu (co-adviser, ECNU).

I'm interested in differential geometry and geometric analysis, especially index theory, minimal surfaces, harmonic functions, and their applications to mathematical general relativity and geometry and topology of positive scalar curvature.
